AREAS OF INTEREST, EXPERTISE AND RESEARCH

Area of Interest

Cognitive and Behavioral Neuroscience

Area of Expertise

Brain signal and image processing, Neuroimaging Techniques i.e., EEG and fMRI

Area of Research

The primary objective of my research is to understand normal brain functioning versus brain disorders to improve predictive accuracy and develop a non-invasive and non-pharmacological intervention. My research methods include clinical, behavioral, neuropsychological, electrophysiological, functional, and structural neuroimaging measures utilizing univariate and multivariate approaches. I mainly apply it in two themes: understanding variabilities in an individual with a brain disorder against the normal (theme 1); and how it can be assisted and improved (theme 2).
