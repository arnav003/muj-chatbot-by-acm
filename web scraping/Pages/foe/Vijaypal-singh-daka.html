CURRENT ACADEMIC ROLE & RESPONSIBILITIES

    Prof. V S Dhaka is a seasoned academician with a flair for entrepreneurial spirit. He enjoys a persistent passion for continuous learning for self and students’ growth. He has more than 16 years of experience in the software industry, academics, research, teaching, and training. His continuous diligence in refining Academic Quality, Building Research Acumen, Developing Entrepreneurial Culture, Improving Experiential Learning and Creating Fostering Culture for innovation has facilitated students of Manipal University Jaipur in achieving laurels at various platforms. Further, he supervises all academic/examination related tasks of the Department of CCE as its Head. So far 12 scholars have successfully obtained their Ph.D degree under his supervision. He is also executing DST sponsored research projects, supervising Ph.D. research scholars and many funded student projects so that strong concepts can lead to great professional skills.
