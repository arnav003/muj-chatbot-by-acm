CURRENT ACADEMIC ROLE & RESPONSIBILITIES


    Prof. Sunil Kumar is a seasoned academician with a keen interest in research and administration. He has more than 18 years of experience in academics, and research. He is dedicated to his students and keen to work on their strengths and potentials. He is heading department examination cell since its inception. He is involved in betterment of academic programs being faculty coordinator, board of studies. He is actively involved in research, currently guiding 05 PhD scholars. He is active in exploring student’s talent and mentoring student technical club ‘Turing Sapiens’
