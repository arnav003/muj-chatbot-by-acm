IoT based smart water consumption and billing management through cloud platform for residential apartments

Inventors (Sandeep Chaurasia, Mahesh Jangid, Harish SHARMA, Varuni Sharma, Shreyansh Shrey, Sourabh Singh Verma) (Australian) Patent Application No. 2021105879 http://pericles.ipaustralia.gov.au/ols/auspat/applicationDetails.do?applicationNo=2021105879 Description: The present invention in smart water management. Smart water meters are Internet of Things (IoT)-enabled devices that allow water users to measure their exact water consumption using a pay-per-use system. The installation of a smart water meter favors dynamic water billing, which removes the need for manual supervision in each household every month. A smart water meter also allows for a two-way communication between water distributors and consumers, allowing the suppliers to track each household's water consumption in real time. By combining appropriate processes, this results in harnessing over-consumption or water wastage. Setting a tariff based on water usage enables the authorities to generate bills and send them to all customers within the timeframe stipulated.

System and Method for Mitigating Collision Risk for A Pedestrian Wearing headphones

Inventors (Sourabh Singh Verma, Santosh Kumar Vishwakarma, Sunil Kumar) (Indian) Patent Application No. 202111045935 https://patentscope.wipo.int/search/en/detail.jsf?docId=IN340501900&_cid=P20-LJOGJT-26671-1 Description: A method for mitigating collision risk for a pedestrian wearing headphones is disclosed. The method comprises capturing one or more images of at least one of a vehicle approaching the pedestrian from rear using one or more cameras fitted within the headphones. The method further comprises analyzing the one or more captured images using a deep learning model to determine an attribute corresponding to the approaching vehicle. Based on the analyzed one or more captured images one or more alert signals are generated on the headphones.
