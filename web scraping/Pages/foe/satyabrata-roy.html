CURRENT ACADEMIC ROLE & RESPONSIBILITIES

    SATYABRATA ROY received the B.Tech. and M.Tech. degrees in CSE from the Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology, West Bengal (Formerly known as West Bengal University of Technology), and KIIT University, Bhubaneswar, respectively. He has completed his PhD from Manipal University Jaipur in the area of lightweight security for IoT.  He is having more than ten years of teaching and research experience. He is currently an Associate Professor with the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Manipal University Jaipur. His major research interests include cryptography and cellular automata. He has published many research papers in referred journals and international conferences. Currently he is supervising three research scholars at Manipal University Jaipur.

    He is a senior member of IEEE, professional member of ACM.

    His research profile can be found at the following sites:

    • ORCID
    • Google Scholar
    • Research Gate

    Key Contributions:

    • Convener of International conferences: ICICV-2020, ICICV-2021.

    • Coordinator of AMS during 2015 – 2019.
    • Core committee member of NIRF from CSE department: 2017, 2018 & 2019.
    • Core committee member of NAAC, NBA and other KPI related activities.
    • Member of BoS, DRC, Curriculum development, PAC.
    • Registration coordinator of the CSE department.
    • Question paper moderation committee member from CSE department.
    • Coordinator of M. Tech. (Information Security).
