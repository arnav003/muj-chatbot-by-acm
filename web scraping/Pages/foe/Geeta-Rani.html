CURRENT ACADEMIC ROLE & RESPONSIBILITIES

    Dr Geeta Rani is working as Associate Professor in the Department of Computer and Communication Engineering at the School of Computer & Communication Engineering., MUJ.She is involved in conducting the theory as well as lab classes for B.Tech. She is working as an active member in designing a new course for the department, syllabus revision committee, and BOS. She is also involved in coordinating the FDP under AICTE. She is responsible for documentations recorded for meetings conducted in the department. She is coordinating the activities of the club ‘Turing Sapiens’ run by CCE and open to the University. She is active in establishing collaborations with industries and medical field to involve students in interdisciplinary projects.
