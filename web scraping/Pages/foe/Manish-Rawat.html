CURRENT ACADEMIC ROLE & RESPONSIBILITIES

    Dr. Manish Rawat is currently an Assistant Professor with the Discipline of Mechatronics Engineering at the Manipal University Jaipur (MUJ). He is associated with Industrial Engineering Research Group at Indian Institute of Technology Indore. He graduated in Industrial and Production Engineering from IITGGU Bilaspur (CG) and later did his masters in Maintenance Engineering from Maulana Azad National Institute of Technology Bhopal (NIT Bhopal). Subsequently, he earned his Ph.D. in the area of Reliability Engineering from the Department of Mechanical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Indore (IITI). Before joining IIT Indore he was working with SR Group of Institution Jhansi (UP) as assistant professor in mechanical engineering department. His research interests are: Reliability Engineering, Fleet Maintenance Planning. He has published several research articles in international journals and conferences of repute.
