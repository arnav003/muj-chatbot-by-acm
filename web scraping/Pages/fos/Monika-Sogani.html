CURRENT ACADEMIC ROLE & RESPONSIBILITIES

    Dr Monika Sogani is a Associate Professor (Senior Scale) in the Department of Bio Sciences, School of Basis Science, Manipal University Jaipur.

    She is also:

    • Teaching curricular subjects and development of teaching content
    • Academic research and consultancy
    • PhD Co-ordinator of the department (2015-2017)
    • Participates in the development of the Curriculum and Programs within the department
    • Member of Directorate of International Collaborations (DoIC) at MUJ
    • Co-Convener, IHE (Delft, Netherlands)-MUJ Joint PG Programme in Non Sewered Sanitation supported by Bill and Malinda Gates Foundation (BMGF), USA
    • Guides students and serves as their Mentors and Tutor Guardians
    • Engages in scholarly and extracurricular activities
